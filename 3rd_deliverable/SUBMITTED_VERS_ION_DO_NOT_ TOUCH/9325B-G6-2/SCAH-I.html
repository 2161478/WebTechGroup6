Our Vision

The SCAH community believes that every child has a different and equally important role in a society. We are zealous about ensuring that every child develops a sense of belonging, significance and purpose through an inclusive community.

Our Mission

SCAH-"I" is a website that hopes to make a difference in the South Central Aurora Hill community by promoting inclusiveness through including everyone in barangay activities such as events and festivities. SCAH-"I" makes this possible by information dissemination and engagement with the community regarding ways to help or assist with inclusive education.

SCAH has three main beliefs that guides us to our goal:

Our Beliefs

1. Children differently abled have the right to live their lives.
2. Children differently abled have the right to proper education and opportunities.
3. Children differently abled are not liabilities but are opportunities to the betterment of society.
